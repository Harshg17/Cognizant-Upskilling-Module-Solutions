Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

Visit the Registration section on the portal, fill in your name, email, preferred date, and select an event type. Click "Register Now" to complete your registration.

Can I change my event preference?

Yes. Select a new event type from the dropdown and your preference will be saved automatically. Use "Clear Saved Preferences" to reset.

How does geolocation work?

Click "Find Nearby Events" and allow your browser to access your location. The portal will then display community events near you.

How do I submit feedback?

Scroll to the Feedback section, select the event you attended, rate it using the star rating, write your feedback, and click Submit.

Who can I contact for support?

Use the Contact section on the portal or email us at events@cityhub.gov.in.
